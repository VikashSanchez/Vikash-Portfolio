About Me

Hello! I’m passionate about graphic and web design with over 5 years of experience in digital design, with a focus on creating intuitive, visually appealing designs. I enjoy turning ideas into functional, user-friendly designs while constantly learning new technologies to enhance my skills. My goal is to blend creativity with problem-solving, continuously evolving as a web and graphic designer to craft innovative, high-quality digital experiences.

I specialize in brand identity, graphic design, and digital experiences. I believe that every pixel should serve a purpose and every color should evoke an emotion.

When I'm not designing, you can find me doing photography, playing various sports, in the gym, or drinking way too much coffee.
